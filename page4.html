If you choose to enter free-form-text, clicking Add Goods/Services initiates a dropdown chart, to which you can add more classes of goods and services, as seen directly below.  

You can choose multiple classes, but must enter a description for each class and pay an additional fee per class. 

To properly fill out the chart, a choice of filing basis [labeled as "Section _"] must be chosen for each item in the table. Clicking a choice of filing basis initiates another section directly below. The two relevant sections [1(a) and 1(b)] are shown below. Sections 44(d) and 44(e) are for international trademarks now being filed in the USA.
